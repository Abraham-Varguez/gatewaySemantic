The City of Pasadena

Why Pasadena?

Pasadena, the most serene and tranquil place in California. Founded in 1873 which was first named "Rancho San Pasqual" which later became to be known as Pasadena, which is a wonderful place to live! Monthly extravagant events that take place in various locations around the city. Pasadena is deeply rich with Art & Culture. Come join one of the most beautiful cities in California. Escape to the best kept secret in Southern California

Pasadena comes from a phrase that belongs to the Ojibwa (Chippewa) Indian language which translates to "Crown of the Valley" and "Key of the Valley" which is why that was stated in our official city seal
